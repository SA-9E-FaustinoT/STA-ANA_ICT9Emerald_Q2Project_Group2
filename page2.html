Designs of Classrooms

Welcome to classrooms where design meets education! Created by the NAZARENO/LICHAUCO team, our spaces bring Dr. Maria Montessori's vision to life with materials and layouts that encourage independent learning, teamwork, creativity, and hands-on exploration, all the ingredients for student success.

Casa Classrooms

Discover a natural sanctuary! Casa classrooms use basic shapes and soothing colors to recreate the feeling of being outdoors, with gentle nods to trees, soil, and sunlight that make learning feel both peaceful and exciting.

Primary Classrooms

Enter a living ecosystem! Primary classrooms transform into conceptual biospheres where water, earth, and air elements come alive on the walls, enhanced by cleverly placed mirrors that catch the light like bursts of sunshine.

Intermediate Classrooms

Walk with the innovators! These classrooms highlight scientific trailblazers from da Vinci's Renaissance genius to Tesla's electrical wonders and Letitia Mumford Geer's medical innovations, a timeline of brilliance that inspires curious minds.

Junior High Classrooms

Explore design from around the world! Junior High spaces feature standout creations including Zaha Hadid's architectural wonders, the iconic Philippine jeepney, and the Eames' classic furniture, perfect for sparking design inspiration and cultural conversation.

Senior High Classrooms

Celebrate Filipino artistic excellence! Each Senior High classroom pays tribute to contemporary masters: Roberto Chabet, Nina Saguil, and Leo Valledor, creating inspiring environments where students can appreciate art history while developing their own creative voices.
